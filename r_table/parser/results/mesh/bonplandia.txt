41941394|This article is the continuation of a previous one by the same author who studies the Wichi phytonymy of shrubs and trees of the Chaco forests in Salta province, Argentina. On this opportunity, a list of 90 Wich names for 83 species of herbs and climbing plants that grow in the same study area is shown and analysed both linguistically and semantically. The most significant results are noted and they are discussed with those of the previous article. In this way, we intend to contribute to the knowledge of Wich phytonymy and to continue discovering nomenclatural patterns in their ethnobotanical lexicon.
