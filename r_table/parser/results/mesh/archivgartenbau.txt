Over a period of three years, in five commercial farms harvested onions were sampled at certain points of the fully mechanized farm harvesting process (twostage harvesting). The material was then cured, prepared and stored over winter in a common storage under equal conditions (about 150 days, mean temperature +2.48 ... + 3.86 C).
